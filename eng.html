About us

Boat mechanic service Moras is a reputable service that operates over 30 years. We are the official service for all volvo pentas, mercruiser, mercury and tohatshu marine engines.

We are located in the main port on the island of Rab, and we do active breakdowns in the port itself or work in the field, which is possible in any dry marina on the island.

The main goal of this service is to facilitate your sea experience.
